WHAT ARE WE PASSIONATE ABOUT?

EarthGlow Energy has a 40-year proud history of delivering power and energy solutions for our customers, safely and reliably.

For over 40 years we have serviced mining companies and State-owned utilities across Australia, developing a reputation as a trusted partner at the forefront of clean energy solutions and the latest technology.

Operating at more than 47 national sites, EarthGlow Energy currently has over 694MW under contract, making us a market leader by scale in the provision of remote, off-grid energy in Australia.
