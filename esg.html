Safety & Health

Safety is our number one priority. We are committed to protecting the health, safety and wellbeing of our people, clients, contractors and the communities we work in. With a focus on improving our knowledge and systems to maintain a safe working environment.

Safety is everyone’s responsibility at EarthGlow Energy; our leaders are focused on maintaining a positive safety culture through ongoing training, communication and improvements of our safety systems.

EarthGlow Energy prides itself on our safety-first approach and ensuring compliance with all applicable regulations for our industry, EarthGlow Energy Kewdale is accredited under the Australian Government Building and Construction WHS Accreditation Scheme and is certified in the following:

  • ISO 14001:2015 Environmental Management Systems
  • ISO 9001:2015 Quality Management Systems
  • AS/NZS 4801:2001 Occupational Health and Safety Management Systems
  • Federal Safety Commissioner WHS Accreditation

Gumala Aboriginal Corporation Partnership

EarthGlow Energy, together with its integrated renewable subsidiary, Hybrid Systems Australia (HSA), has a joint three-year Memorandum of Understanding (MOU) with the Gumala Aboriginal Corporation (GAC).

GAC is one of the largest Aboriginal corporations, serving Banjima, Yinhawangka and Nyiyaparli people of the Pilbara region in Western Australia.

Under the MOU, GAC will identify remote communities in the Pilbara for the introduction of sustainable, renewable energy, reducing reliance of fossil fuel generators. Hybrid Systems is converting those remote communities to hybrid power by incorporating solar and battery storage with existing generators through the installation of Hybrid Systems’ industry leading Standalone Power Systems (SPS).

“We are delighted to have entered this partnership with GAC and to be able to utilise our skills and experience in remote, hybrid energy generation to add to the incredible work and initiatives that GAC undertakes to benefit the lives and futures of the Banjima, Yinhawangka and Nyiyaparli Traditional Owners”

“This is a practical, useful and environmentally responsible way that EarthGlow Energy is utilising our specialist capabilities to give back to local communities”
